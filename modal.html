ENDING NOTES

The human subject is not fixed or given to us, it is through self-creation that the self is born.

In a very real sense we've always been technologically augmented. The two really big technologies - language and fire - were around before us and shaped our evolution.

Technology can both reinforce and challenge gender norms, depending on the technologists in charge. It allows people to present themselves authentically through the utilization of digital applications, but it can also disconnect them from the norm; effectively continuing the historical ‘othering’ of some.

Technology can provide a framework for different kinds of subjects, people, to exist. Through the proper consumption of technological devices and the ideals of transhumanism, technology could grant us a posthuman world, and therefore a postgender world.

Will an awareness of the pervasiveness of gendered, or otherwise biased, technology affect the way users interact with tech?
